Jeep Project Title

I upgraded my 1996 Jeep Cherokee with GPS, dash cam, Wifi, TV, and custom music playability features. We got my 1996 Jeep Cherokee during high school for a bargain of just $800. All I needed back then was a vehicle that took me from point A to point B.

After a while, I desired the features that some of the newer cars had. I saw commercials of vehicles with fancy features such as dash cams, music storage, and even in-vehicle Wifi. The $15,000 for a new vehicle was way too expensive for me, so I decided to install these modern features into my Jeep.
